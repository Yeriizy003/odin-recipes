Homemade Oatmeal

Description

It's delicious and tasty oatmeal with brown sugar and maple with some peanut butter to make it more creamy

Ingredients

  • Peanut Butter
  • Oats
  • Maple Syrup
  • Brown Sugar
  • Milk

Steps

  1. Mix the oats with the sugar while dry, then put milk in
  2. Put it in microwave for 1 to 2 minutes
  3. Then put the rest of ingredients in til desired amount
