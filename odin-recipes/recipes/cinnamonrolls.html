Cinnamon Roll

Description

It's a sweet roll with icing and cinnamon sugar stuffed in between the rolls of the roll.

Ingredients

  • Cinnamon
  • Eggs
  • Flour
  • Sugar
  • Yeast

Steps

  1. Mix flour, eggs, and yeast
  2. Let the mixture rest rise for half an hour
  3. Mix the Cinnamon and sugar together
  4. lay a strip of dough out and put the Cinnamon Sugar mixture over the top
  5. Roll the stip of dough up
  6. Let it bake for 30 mintutes
